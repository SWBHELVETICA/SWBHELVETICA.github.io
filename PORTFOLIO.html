.correct

this is a work of irony.
in turning your brand into an anachronism
it adopts the quality of prophecy

what's curious to me
is the context of
cars and art in competition and context of time
the 3.5 CSL was used in racing during the same year Warhol's Brigitte Bardot was made
both in 1974

in removing the word
'period'
the philosophy of the brand has new meaning
to be correct, always correct
not in that you are never wrong
but only in that you are free
we are forever worthy
of our own potential

by taking an event that belongs to history
and imbuing it with humor
and branding
you can make it yours.
by giving context to laughter
you can turn gossip into art

these were all studies of reappropriation
by taking something that has always existed
and giving it new life
you can make something nobody has ever seen

because these designs were made with my personal curiousity and philosophy, the process of creation and design is recursive in its generation. there is no beginning or end, only the many iterations of a singular yet complex idea. if they are cool or interesting it is only because the impetus has any momentum. nothing is post-rationalized.
